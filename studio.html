The studio

A collective of thirty architects, interior designers and makers who believe that good architecture is felt long before it is seen.

01Philosophy

Architecture, to us, is an act of subtraction. We remove until only the essential remains — then we refine the essential until it feels inevitable.

We work slowly and deliberately, favouring a small number of projects each year so that every decision receives the attention it deserves.

Material is our language. Concrete, timber, stone and glass are chosen not for effect but for how they weather, hold light and register the passage of time.

02Approach

How we work.

01

Listen

Every commission begins with immersion — in the site, the light, the client's life and the culture of place. We design nothing until we understand everything.

02

Distil

We reduce complexity to a single organising idea — a concept clear enough to guide every subsequent decision, from massing to the grain of a handrail.

03

Craft

Working hand-in-hand with engineers and makers, we prototype, test and refine until detail and whole speak with one voice.

04

Endure

We deliver buildings designed to age gracefully — to become more themselves, not less, with every passing decade.
