00About Us

At Suraj Mishra Architects, we believe architecture is more than constructing buildings—it is about creating meaningful experiences, enriching lifestyles, and designing spaces that connect people with their environment.

Based in Sambalpur, Odisha, our studio specializes in contemporary, sustainable, and context-sensitive architecture that seamlessly blends functionality with aesthetics. Every project is approached with a deep understanding of the client's aspirations, site conditions, culture, and future needs.

From elegant residences to commercial spaces and institutional projects, our team is committed to delivering designs that inspire and endure.

Our design philosophy revolves around:

  • Human-Centric Design
  • Sustainable Architecture
  • Innovative Spatial Planning
  • Timeless Aesthetics
  • Contextual and Climate-Responsive Solutions
00Our Vision

To become a leading architectural practice recognized for creating innovative, sustainable, and transformative spaces that positively impact communities and enhance the quality of life.

Our Mission

  • To deliver thoughtful and functional architectural solutions.
  • To integrate sustainability and technology into every project.
  • To provide personalized design experiences to every client.
  • To create timeless spaces that reflect identity and purpose.
